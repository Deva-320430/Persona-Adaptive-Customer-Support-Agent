# Password Reset Guide

## Steps to Reset Your Password

1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link (valid for 30 minutes)
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Password must be at least 10 characters, with one number and one symbol

## Common Issues

**Reset email not received**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Confirm the email address is correct and matches your account
- Wait up to 5 minutes; emails can be delayed during high traffic
- If still not received after 10 minutes, the account may be using SSO (Google/Microsoft login) and won't have a password to reset

**Account locked after failed attempts**
- After 5 failed login attempts, the account locks for 15 minutes automatically
- Locked accounts cannot request a password reset during the lockout window
- After 3 separate lockout events in 24 hours, manual support intervention is required

**Reset link expired or invalid**
- Reset links expire after 30 minutes and are single-use
- Request a new link from the login page

# Refund Policy

## Eligible for Refund
- Cancellation within 14 days of initial signup (full refund, no questions asked)
- Confirmed duplicate or erroneous charge caused by a system error
- Service outage exceeding the SLA commitment (issued as service credit, not cash refund, unless customer explicitly requests cash and case is approved by billing lead)

## Not Eligible for Refund
- Dissatisfaction with the product after the 14-day window
- Failure to cancel before a renewal date ("I forgot to cancel")
- Partial month usage when downgrading or cancelling mid-cycle

## Refund Process
1. Customer requests refund via support
2. Support verifies eligibility against the criteria above
3. Eligible refunds under $200 can be processed directly by support
4. Refunds over $200, or any case not clearly matching the eligible criteria, must be escalated to the billing team lead for manager approval

## Escalation Criteria
Always escalate when: the customer mentions a chargeback or dispute already filed with their bank, the refund amount exceeds $200, or the situation doesn't clearly fit the eligible/not-eligible categories above.

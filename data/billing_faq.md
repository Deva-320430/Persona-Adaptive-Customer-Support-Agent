# Billing FAQ

## Subscription Plans
- Starter: $29/month, up to 5 users, 10,000 API calls/month
- Professional: $99/month, up to 25 users, 100,000 API calls/month
- Enterprise: custom pricing, unlimited users, dedicated support

## Billing Cycle
- Charges occur on the same calendar day each month as the original signup date
- Annual plans are billed once per year with a 15% discount versus monthly billing
- Failed payments trigger 3 retry attempts over 7 days before account suspension

## Common Billing Questions

**Why was I charged twice?**
This usually happens when a plan upgrade is processed mid-cycle. The system charges a prorated amount for the upgrade immediately, separate from the regular monthly charge. This is expected behavior, not a duplicate charge, but should be explained clearly since it looks like an error.

**Can I get a refund?**
Refunds are only issued for: (a) duplicate accidental charges confirmed as a system error, (b) cancellation within 14 days of initial signup, (c) service outages exceeding our SLA commitment (99.5% uptime). Refund requests outside these categories require manager approval and should be escalated.

**How do I downgrade my plan?**
Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Usage in excess of the new plan's limits before the downgrade date will not be penalized retroactively.

## Escalation Criteria
Any billing dispute involving amounts over $500, any mention of "chargeback," "dispute with my bank," or "lawsuit," or any refund request outside the standard policy above should be escalated to the billing team lead immediately.
